Sponsorship

Sponsoring BarCamp is a great way to support Birmingham’s vibrant tech and creative community.

We accept contributions in any amount, but the following is suggested:

  • $250 – Silver Level
  • $500 – Gold Level

We recognize sponsors in the following ways:

Gold

  • logo, link and description on BarCamp website under Gold
  • recognition in social media and email to BarCamp attendees
  • company signs at registration area at the event
  • Logo on T-shirt (provided enough funds)

Silver

  • logo, link and description on BarCamp website under Silver
  • recognition in social media and email to BarCamp attendees
  • company name on T-shirt (provided enough funds)

In-kind sponsorship: You can also sponsor specific event functions

  • catering lunch
  • coffee and snacks for breakfast
  • and more...

We also encourage sponsors to attend and fully participate in BarCamp. There is no better way to be noticed by our community.
